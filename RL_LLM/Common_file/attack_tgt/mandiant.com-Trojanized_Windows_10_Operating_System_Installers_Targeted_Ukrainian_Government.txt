 1. Threat actor UNC4166 trojanized Windows 10 installers with Ukrainian language packs. 2. The installers were distributed via torrent sites and installed by Ukrainian government targets. 3. Installers contained scheduled tasks that profiled systems and communicated with TOR C2 servers. 4. UNC4166 determined if targets were of interest for further exploitation. 5. Additional malware was deployed on selected systems for reconnaissance and data theft, including backdoors like SPAREPART, BEACON, and STOWAWAY. 6. Backdoors enabled further access, command execution, and data exfiltration from compromised networks.
