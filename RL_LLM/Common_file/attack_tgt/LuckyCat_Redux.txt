1. The attackers register doppelganger domains mimicking the victims' organizations. 2. They create fake websites on these domains copied from the real organizations' websites. 3. Phishing emails are sent to targets directing them to the fake websites. 4. Victims visit the fake websites and enter login credentials, which are captured by the attackers. 5. The credentials are used to gain unauthorized access to the real websites and further infiltrate the victims' networks.
