1. The attackers sent malicious links through KakaoTalk, Facebook, and email. 2. The links led to Android apps that acted as droppers. 3. The droppers tricked users into granting accessibility permissions. 4. The droppers installed a spyware Trojan on the device. 5. The Trojan used cloud services for command and control functions. 6. It gathered device information and exfiltrated data like messages and contacts. 7. Variants were seen that used different cloud services or dropped additional malware. 8. The campaign targeted North Korean defectors, journalists, and related groups.
