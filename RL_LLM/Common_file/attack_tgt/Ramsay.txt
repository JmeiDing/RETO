1. Weaponized Office documents exploiting vulnerabilities are used to deliver Ramsay. 2. Ramsay establishes persistence using techniques like hijacking DLLs loaded by services. 3. Ramsay searches for and collects Word documents from local and removable drives. 4. Collected documents are encrypted and hidden inside normal .doc files. 5. Ramsay looks for control files on removable media to execute commands from its operators. 6. The spreading component infects EXE files on removable drives to propagate further. 7. Ramsay can scan the local network for hosts vulnerable to EternalBlue for lateral movement. 8. An external component is used to exfiltrate the encrypted documents stored inside normal .doc files.
