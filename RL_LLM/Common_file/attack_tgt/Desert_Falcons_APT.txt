 1. The attackers prepare spear phishing emails with political or news themes as lures to target victims. 2. If targets open attachments, they infect victims with the Falcons Trojan or DHS Spyware malware. 3. The malware establishes communication with Desert Falcons command and control servers. 4. Falcons operators categorize victims into groups and assign operators to monitor them. 5. The malware sends stolen data like files, screenshots, and keylogs to the C2 servers. 6. Operators browse the C2 panels to collect interesting files and data from victims. 7. Based on victim importance, the attackers either intensify monitoring or drop surveillance. 8. The malware continues receiving commands from the C2 server for further activities. 9. Separate campaigns with different malware target victims in Egypt, Israel, Palestine, etc.
