1. Worok compromises websites and leverages exploits like ProxyShell to gain initial access. 2. They establish persistence using webshells like ReGeorg. 3. Worok deploys reconnaissance tools like Mimikatz and NBTScan for internal discovery. 4. They deploy the CLRLoad or PowHeartBeat first stage loaders. 5. The loaders retrieve and execute the PNGLoad second stage payload. 6. PNGLoad searches for PNG images containing hidden payloads. 7. It extracts the payloads from the images and decrypts them. 8. The decrypted PowerShell scripts are executed for further malicious actions. 9. PowHeartBeat is used to communicate with C2 servers to receive commands and exfiltrate data.
