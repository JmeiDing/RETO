1. The attackers send messages over WhatsApp impersonating a think tank and containing malicious links. 2. The target clicks on the link which directs them to a fake login page mimicking Microsoft, Google, or Yahoo. 3. The target enters their login credentials on the fake page, which are captured by the attackers. 4.
