1. Sandworm compromised the IT network of a Ukrainian energy provider. 2. On April 8th at 2:58pm UTC, the attackers deployed a worm script that propagated through SSH using credentials. 3. The worm scheduled a wiper malware variant for Linux or Solaris to activate at 5:58pm UTC on infected systems. 4. At 4:10pm UTC on April 8th, Sandworm executed Industroyer2 malware on a system to impact industrial control systems. 5. Industroyer2 communicated with devices using the IEC-104 protocol to cut power. 6. At 4:20pm UTC, the attackers ran CaddyWiper on the same system that ran Industroyer2 to cover their tracks.
