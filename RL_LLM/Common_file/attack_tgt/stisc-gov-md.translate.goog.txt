1. The attackers send phishing emails impersonating domain name registrars. 2. The emails claim domain names are expiring and provide a link to renew them. 3. The link leads to a fake payment page to steal financial information. 4. Over 1,330 such emails have been sent to government institutions in Moldova. 5. Recipients are urged not to click on links or provide any sensitive information. 6. The phishing emails aim to trick recipients into entering credentials or payment data. 7. Stolen data would enable the attackers to gain access to accounts or make fraudulent transactions. 8. The phishing campaign is part of an ongoing wave of cyberattacks in the country. 9. Awareness is being raised to help recipients identify and avoid the phishing threats. 10. Basic cybersecurity practices like verifying senders and links are recommended.
