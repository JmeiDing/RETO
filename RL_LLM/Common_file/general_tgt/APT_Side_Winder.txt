The report analyzes a malicious RTF document targeting Vietnamese officials that exploits CVE-2017-11882. The infection chain drops files RasTls.dll and dascgosrky.exe on the system after decrypting and decompressing payloads. Dascgosrky.exe hijacks RasTls.dll to load malicious code. The infrastructure and TTPs match previous activity by the Chinese group 1937CN that has targeted Vietnam before. Domains and IP addresses associated with the attack are linked to previous 1937CN campaigns. The analysis reveals that the attacker uses RTF files to make the intrusion and dll hijacking to load the real payload. The toolset used by the group to create the RTF malicious document has the same properpy of the SideWinder.
