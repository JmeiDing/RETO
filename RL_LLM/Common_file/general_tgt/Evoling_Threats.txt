Initial phishing campaign targets officials attending an airshow, delivering malware. Malware provides initial foothold on systems. Lateral movement through the network to compromise additional systems. Exploitation of vulnerabilities like MS14-068 to escalate privileges. Compromise of Active Directory and highly classified enclaves. Deployment of custom malware like Chopstick RAT for spying and data exfiltration. Use of techniques like credential dumping to maintain persistence in the environment.
