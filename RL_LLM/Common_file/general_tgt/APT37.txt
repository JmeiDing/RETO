APT37 (Reaper), also known as The Overlooked North Korean Actor, is a suspected North Korean cyber espionage group. FireEye believes that this threat actor is working on behalf of the North Korean government and targets South Korea, Japan, Vietnam, the Middle East, and various industry verticals. Tactics include social engineering, web compromises, torrents to distribute malware. APT37 has access to zero-day vulnerabilities in Hangul Word Processor and Adobe Flash, as well as compromised servers, messaging platforms, and cloud service providers for command and control infrastructure. They use a diverse suite of malware for initial intrusion and exfiltration, along with custom malware used for espionage purposes.
