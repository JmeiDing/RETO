This PDF document, titled "APT28.pdf", is a report by FireEye on the Russian cyber espionage group APT28. The report was published in January 2017 and provides an overview of APT28's targeting and intrusion activity. It includes information on the group's tactics, such as using flexible modular frameworks, leveraging zero-day vulnerabilities, compromising legitimate websites to infect visitors, and gaining access to organizations by compromising their web-facing servers. The report also discusses APT28's involvement in various political events, including the 2016 Olympics and Paralympics doping scandal, public accusations of state-sponsored hacking, and the 2016 US presidential election. The authors conclude that APT28 continues to evolve its toolkit and refine its tactics in an effort to protect its operational effectiveness in the face of increased scrutiny.
