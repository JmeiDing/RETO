The PDF describes a cyber espionage campaign targeting government organizations in the Middle East by a threat actor group called Emissary Panda. The attackers exploited a vulnerability in SharePoint servers to install webshells and used them to upload hacking tools to move laterally and collect data from compromised networks. Analysis of the tools reveals overlaps with previous Emissary Panda campaigns. The report provides technical analysis of the tactics, techniques and procedures of this attack, including the custom malware payloads deployed. It recommends protections through threat intelligence, network monitoring, and endpoint detection to defend against this threat actor.
